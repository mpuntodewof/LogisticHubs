# NiagaOne Storefront — Stitch.ai Prompts for Figma Prototype

## Design System Context

Use this context as a prefix for ALL prompts below:

**Brand:** NiagaOne — Indonesian mid-market retail e-commerce platform
**Style:** Modern, clean, mobile-first, professional and trustworthy
**Primary color:** Deep blue (#1E3A5F) — trust, professionalism
**Accent color:** Warm orange (#F5841F) — energy, CTA buttons
**Neutral:** Light gray (#F5F5F5) background, white cards
**Typography:** Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans (modern, supports Latin + Indonesian characters)
**Language:** Bahasa Indonesia as primary (use realistic Indonesian text, not Lorem Ipsum)
**Target user:** Indonesian consumer, 25-55 years old, shopping on mobile and desktop
**Product type:** General hard goods retail store (electronics, power tools, home appliances, automotive parts, lighting & electrical, hand tools, accessories)
**Store name for prototype:** "Orbix Store" — a mid-market Indonesian hard goods retailer with 3 physical branches and an online store
**Currency:** IDR with titik separator (e.g., Rp 250.000)
**Breakpoints:** Mobile 375px, Tablet 768px, Desktop 1440px
